Fireblast
Requires: Proficiency (Fire) I
Effect: Throw Spellpacket for Fire 1 damage
Cooldown: Incant " the fire and cracks
Of sulphurous roaring the most mighty Neptune
Seem to besiege and make his bold waves tremble,
Yea, his dread trident shake."

Fireball

Requires: Proficiency (Fire) II
Effect: Throw a Spellpacket for Fire 2 damage blast
Cooldown: Incant: "These violent delights have violent ends
And in their triumph die, like fire and powder
Which, as they kiss, consume."

Enchanct Weapon 
Requires: Proficiency (Fire) II
Effect: The next attack made by target ally is type fire and does +2 damage. Only one may be active at a time.
Cooldown: Incant: "Caesar's spirit, ranging for revenge,
With Ate by his side come hot from hell,
Shall in these confines with a monarch's voice
Cry 'Havoc,' and let slip the dogs of war."
